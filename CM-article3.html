November 11, 2021

Castillo snags Top 9th in Oct-Nov 2021 Physician Licensure Exam

Monica Mica B. Castillo brings immense pride to her alma mater, Our Lady of Fatima University (OLFU), after landing in the Top 9th rank in the recent October-November 2021 Physician Licensure Exam.

The Professional Regulation Commission declares that Castillo garnered a score of 86.58%, as well as a fact that OLFU registered a total of 138 passers in the said exam.

The University shares in the joy and triumph of Dr. Castillo and the latest batch of newly-licensed physicians, along with their parents, guardians, and loved ones.
