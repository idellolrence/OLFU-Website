February 24, 2022

Psych Grad Dominates as Top 1 in Feb 22 Psychometrician Exam

The OLFU Antipolo Campus is literally walking on cloud nine. Their alumnus, Joshua James B. Dela Cruz of the Psychology Department of the College of Arts and Sciences, took the Top 1 spot in the nation in the February 2022 Psychometrician Licensure Examination.

Official results released by the Professional Regulation Commission show that Dela Cruz bested the 2,240 others who took the test in which only 969 (including Dela Cruz) made it to the passing mark.

OLFU celebrates its total of 41 passers from its campuses in Antipolo, Valenzuela, Quezon City, and Pampanga. Cheers to the University’s newly minted Psychometricians!
