March 04, 2022

SMART Balik Tuition Promo 2 awards 100k to Med Student, Plus 50 Pocket Wifi's

Holding true to its promise in the SMART Balik Tuition Promo 2, SMART Communications Inc. followed through when their executives visited OLFU on 03 March 2022 for a formal turnover of prizes.

At the OLFU’s RISE Tower at the Valenzuela Campus, SMART awarded Php 100,000 to Ariel Abantes (present virtually) who is a 2nd Year student at OLFU’s College of Medicine. Additionally, since Abantes attends OLFU, SMART handed over 50 units of its Smart Bro Pocket Wifi’s to the University.

Present during the turnover event from SMART were its team members from its Community Solutions and Business Development segment, namely: Louie de Guzman, AVP and Head: Roland Garcia, NCR Head Manager, OLFU executives present were: Dean Ernesto Leuterio Jr., Campus Administrator-Valenzuela Campus; Rafael Enriquez, Registrar-College of Medicine, and Vicente Santos III, Head-Social Media and Digital Marketing.
