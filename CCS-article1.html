March 26, 2022

Art Matters: New Era of Multimedia—A CCS-Huion Collaboration

CCS Technocrats from all levels attended a webinar pertaining to the contemporary state of Multimedia across borders and channels, powered by technology. With the theme, "Art Matters: New Era of Multimedia," the students were given key notes of the ever-changing progress in multimedia technology on 26 March, 2022.

The guest speakers of the webinar were talented professionals in the field of multimedia, whose legacy percedes their names and works. Coming from different landmasses, and timezones, the three speakers shared their experiences and valuable abstract knowledge input that the students will valuably integrate as they become professionals in the industry. Elected representatives from Huion Evangelists, they were: Randy Valiente, from Manila, Costis Papatheodorou, from Greece, and Emot Amodia, from Cebu.

Three H64OP Tablets were given up for grabs by the end of the webinar, all sponsored by Huion themselves. The mechanics of winning the devices were based on three trivia questions provided by the end of the congress. Google forms were disseminated which contained the questions, and the first three students to have guessed the answers earned their rewards. The three winners were Abriel Christian Bulaon, first-year EMC, Joessa Lyka Beliza, second-year EMC, and Paolo Rosales, senior high school.

Huion is a technology-centered brand catering to digital tablets for illustrations and animations. It is a product of Shenzhen Huion Animation Technology Co., Ltd., which was established in 2011. As a national high-tech enterprise with numerous patents and independent R&D capabilities, Huion designs, manufactures and markets pen tablets, pen displays, LED light pads and many other interactive handwriting input devices of Kamvas and Inspiroy series. Apart from digital devices, Huion also provides individualized OEM and ODM solutions. Agents of the company locate in various parts of the world, including America, Europe, Australia and most Asian countries.

Warmest gratitude to Huion from the CCS Family for the ecstatic support in this collaboration!
