College of Business and Accountancy

The programs under the College of Business and Accountancy (CBA) aim to equip students with, initially a solid general foundation in business administration, and progressing thereafter to a mastery of concepts, tools and skills needed for advanced managements and financial science. CBA reinforces these with practical exposure in the real business world through its practicum program and industry linkages.

Today, students of the College of Business and Accountancy reap the full benefits from OLFU'S world-class learning platforms and resources which are paired with state of the art facilities and laboratories all delivered by a competent teaching faculty through a masterful mix of learning modalities: online, distance or, when health conditions permit face to face learning.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

Major in Banking

This program aims to equip the students with sound and relevant knowledge and skills in Banking and Finance. It intends to provide a strong foundation in banking operation, investment principles and policies geared towards producing key executives and personnel in banks and other financial institutions. The curriculum allows the students to obtain another diploma in International Business under the Australian Qualifications Framework if the students want to convert some units under AQF.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

Major in Marketing Management

This course provides students with the knoledge and skills necessary in creating effective marketing strategies and marketing program development. It covers all aspects of marketing including, but not limited to, marketing research, strategic marketing, product planning and developoment, information technology, and social media maximation. It equips student with analytical, communication, creativity, and interpersonal skills that are aligned to industry demand. the curriculum allows the students to obtain another diploma in international busisness under the australian qualification framework if the students want to convert some units under AQF.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

Major in Operations and Supply Chain Management

This degree offers a unique opportunity for students to learn operations management from strategic, financial, and business development perspectives. It also includes the use of information tchnology updates in utililizing enterprise resource software. this provides a concrete platform for career in international operations. The curiculum allows the students to obtain another diploma in international busisness under the australian qualification framework if the students want to convert some units under AQF.

Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

This program has a curriculum for students who wish to pursue a professional career in Accountancy. It complies with the international Federation of Accountancy (IFAC) through its implementation of the International education standard that qualifies the graduates of the program to take assessments leading to certification in accountancy given by PRC-BOA and other global professional accountancy organizations. Graduates of this program may engage in assurance and audit services for statutory financial reporting and tax-related services, provide management advisory through accounting expertise in financial reporting and control, and other related services in the accounting field. The curriculum allows the students to obtain another diploma in International Business under the Australian Qualifications Framework if the students want to convert some units under AQF.

Bachelor of Science in Accounting Information System

This course provide general accounting education for students to gain knowledge on the accounting information system. This achieved through the combination of knowledge in business, accounting, and computer system such as System Application and Products in Data Processing (SAP). It also provides excellent training on strengthening professional knowledge, skills, and attitude which enable graduates to continue learning and adapting to changes in their professional lives. Graduates of the program are expected to excel as CFOs (Chief Financial Officers) or as Financial CIOs (Chief Information Officers) with skills in financial planning, controlling, and performance evaluation of the firm through an effective information system. The curriculum allows the students to obtain another diploma in Internationals Business under the Australian Qualifications Framework if the students want to convert some units under AQF.

Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship

This degree develops the entrepreneural mindset, thereby developing students in the application of business concepts and enterprise management. This prepares them for general establishment and management of small and medium business enterprises. After completion of the enterpreneurship program, graduates should be able to set up and operate a business enterprise or work in any organization where enterpreneural competencies are required. This program is currently offered in the Quezon City campus. the curriculum allows the students to obtain another diploma in International Business under the Autralian Qualification Framework if the students want to convert some units under AQF.
