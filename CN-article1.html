December 13, 2021

PRC declares Nursing's Tuburo as Top 4th in the November 2021 Board Exams

Alumnus Dennis Isorena Tuburo of the College of Nursing of Our Lady of Fatima University (OLFU)- Valenzuela Campus was officially declared as having ranked Top 4th in the November 2021 Nursing Licensure Exam (NLE.) His 87.80% rating earned him the enviable spot.

The newly minted Registered Nurse Tuburo took the board exam issued out by the Professional Regulation Commision (PRC) alongside other OLFU Nursing graduates. In all, OLFU celebrates 428 passers.

Thus far, the year 2021 is expected to end with stellar marks in the PRC Board Exams. Kristan Dela Cruz was Top 8th in the Mark 2021 Medical Technologist Licensure Exam, while Anna Clarissa Panes took the Top 7th spot in the July 2021 Nursing Licensure Exam. Then, Monina Mica Castillo wowed in the October-November 2021 Pharmacist Licensure Exam. Finally, OLFU Antipolo also gained distinction in the June 2021 Pharmacist Licensure Exams when it was declared by the PRC as a Top 8 Performing School.

The University takes pride in Dennis I. Tuburo, RN, its 428 new newly licensed Nurses, and all its graduates who have raised the bar in the licensure exams in 2021. Well done!
