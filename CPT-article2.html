PT Researchers awarded as Best Presenters in Impetus 2021

A team of student researchers from the College of Physical Therapy of Our Lady of Fatima University (OLFU) notched a victory when they were proclaimed as the "Best Research Poster Presentation" in the event, IMPETUS 2021: Philippine Physical Therapy Association National Virtual Convention, held on December 04 to 05, 2021.

The compelling research work on "Screen hours, Room, Number of Gadgets and Their Relation to Sleep Quality among Allied Health Students Enrolled in Online Class" is credited to students Carol Chew, Iva Kolin Basilio, Joyce Ann Adap, Joshua Angelo Balmes, Myrna Dela Pedra, and Carlos Escabarte, as well as the expert guidance of their Adviser, Maybelle Anne Zamora.

With the convention espousing the theme, "Pushing forward together during the changing times", these young, inquisitive, and analytical minds of OLFU did deliver very relevant results and findings. The University takes pride in their achievement!
