College of Arts and Sciences

Today, students of the College of Arts and Sciences reap the full benefits from OLFU's world-class learning platforms and resources which are paired with state-of-the-art facilities and laboratories- all delivered by a competent teaching faculty through a masterful mix of learning modalitiess: online, distance or, when health conditions permit, face-to-face learning.

It is also noteworthy that OLFU is one among a few select schools to be granted the Certificate of Authority by the CHEd, allowing it to conduct limited face-toface classes in the midst of the COVID- 19 pandemic; a testament to the University's established and effective community safety protocols.

Bachelor of Science in Psychology

The Psychology program foster high quality education in psychological science through effective instruction, research, and community serivce. Its curriculum provides the appropriate breadth and depth in the study of psychology. Paired with a strong practicum exposure in educational industrial, and clinical settings, the course gives gradutes a broad range of transferable skills; ranging from communication, critical thinking, analysis and interpersonal skills, problem solving, data handling, and acaemic writing, which serves as preparation for their future profession.

Bachelor of Science in Biology

The Biology Program prepares learners for a career in the biological sciences field by enhancing their appreciation and understandning of the natural world. This is achieved through the use of the latest scientific tools and techniques in both laboratory and outdoor settings. Graduates acquire knowledge in scientific principles, basic laws, and current theories of biology.

Bachelor of Arts in Communication

The Communication program provides an integrated training for prospective media professionals whose career practice focuses on the management of communication and other information-related functions such as research, analysis, program planning, and development. Students recieve the foundation essential for technical experts and managers of public relations, advertising, learning systems deisgn, and multi-media production for both pulic and corporate settings.

Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science

The Environmental Science program provides an integrated training for professional practice centered on the environment which includes environmental resources, quality assessment, and management for private and goverment institutions. From the perspective of the natural sciences, it fortifies the career preparation of students in the social sciences; giving due emphasis on ethics, society, and culture. Students are equipped with sufficient knowledge on the scientific theories and techniques needed to monitor and understand environmental quality. Graduates gain the ability to interpet various environments, analyze people's behavior and needs, and critique sustainable initiatives.

Career Opportunities

  • Policy and Report Analyst
  • Environmental Technician
  • Coordinator, Educator, and Manager Roles in Different Sectors
