December 14, 2021

OLFU PT Alumni reign supreme as Top 1 and Top 3 in December 2021 Physical Therapy Exam

In the December 2021 results of the Physical Therapy Licensure Exam of the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC), two graduates of Our Lady of Fatima University emerged as topnotchers. Taking the Top 1 spot is Jasmine Lianne B. Esguerra of OLFU Antipolo, and snagging the Top 3 rank is Paulo R. Garvida of OLFU Quezon City.

Esguerra landed her Top 1 placing with her rating of 90.40%, with Garvida following closely with his 88.90% mark which elevated him to the Top 3rd rank.

OLFU also performed significantly better than the national passing rate of 68.97%. OLFU Pampanga delivered a passing average of 84.62% (total test-takers); OLFU Quezon City 77.27%, and OLFU Valenzuela- 71.43%.

The University congratulates its 90 newly licensed Physical Therapists!

Year 2021 has turned out to be another banner year for OLFU in the PRC's licensure exams, having scored multiple topnotchers across several programs. Kristan Dela Cruz was Top 8th in the March 2021 Medical Technologist Licensure Exam, while Anna Clarissa Panes took the Top 7th spot in the July 2021 Nursing Licensure Exam. Then, Monina Mica Castillo wowed in the October-November 2021 Physician Licensure Exams when she listed as Top 9th, and so did Kenneth Betita who finished as Top 6th in the November 2021 Pharmacist Licensure Exam. And, in the November 2021 Nursing Licensure Exam, Dennis I. Tuburo sailed high as the Top 4th out of all examinees. Further, OLFU Antipolo also gained distinction in the June 2021 Pharmacist Licensure Exams when it was declared by the PRC as the Top 8 Performing School.
