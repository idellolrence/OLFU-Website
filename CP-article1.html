PRC proclaims Betita Top 6th in November 2021 Pharmacist Licensure Exam

In the results of the November 2021 Pharmacist Licensure Examination, the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC) revealed Kenneth Honrubia Betita as having taken the Top 6th place among examiness across the country.

Betita is an alumnus from Our Lady of Fatima University (OLFU), specifically of the College of Pharmacy, Quezon City Campus. He scored 91.70% in the tough licensure exam which recorded a national passing average of 50.91%.

The University celebrates its 155 newly licensed Pharmacists!
