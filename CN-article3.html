December 03, 2021

Nursing Researchers cited Best Presenters at ICONS X

Nursing students of Our Lady of Fatima University (Valenzuela Campus) were recently adjudged as Best Research Podium Presenter in the 10th International Conference of Nurses(ICONS X). The research work that earned the recognition was entitled, "COVID-19 Anxiety and Stigma as Moderators of the job Demands and ProQOL among Filipino Nurses". Held virtually on November 26 to 27, 2021, ICONS X was hosted by Cebu Normal University.

The ace research team from OLFU's College of Nursing is composed of 4th Year Students: Carmina Angeli SD. Caparaz, Ma. Andrea Nicole A. Arceo, Lovely Shayne S. Clavite, Ma. Bernie Mae N. Marquez, and Francesca Alysse L. Perez. The graduating students were guided by their Research Advisors, namely: Dr. Michael Joseph Dino, Sharon Cajayon, and Brylle Capili.
